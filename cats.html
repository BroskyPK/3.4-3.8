Cats

Find out everything you need to know about owning a cat or adopting one from Toni's Animal Shelter.

Adopt a Cat

We have a very active rehome programme that produces fantastic results.

We get help from the SPCA, HUHA and other rescue organisations for our cat rehome programme. All our cats are temperament tested to make sure they're suitable for rehoming, microchipped and are required to be desexed (vouchers are available).

What do the adoption costs cover?

Our adoption service costs can include:

  • a $35 vaccination fee
  • a desexing voucher (once you apply - depending on weight and sex of cat)
  • the registration fee for the year (pro-rata for the year)
